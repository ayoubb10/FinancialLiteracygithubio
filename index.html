Introduction

Financial literacy is the ability to understand and use various financial skills, such as budgeting, investing, borrowing, and managing money. It helps people make informed and effective decisions about their finances and achieve their personal and social goals.

Advantages

Financial literacy is the ability to understand and use various financial skills, such as budgeting, saving, investing, and debt management. It can help you make better financial decisions, manage your money and debt effectively, reach your financial goals, reduce your expenses, and cope with less financial stress. Financial literacy can also improve your quality of life and contribute to a stronger economy .

For students

Students can benefit from financial literacy in many ways, such as learning how to manage their money effectively, and avoid financial stress and problems.Besides ,making better financial decisions for their education and career. Financial literacy can help them understand the costs and benefits of different education options, such as scholarships, and work-study programs .
